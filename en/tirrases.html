TIRRASES

Coordinators: Horacio and Esther Lónpez
E-mail: horacio.cpci@gmail.com | esther.cpci@gmail.com

The Tirrases ministry seeks to transform the community through the teaching of the Word and a holistic social approach. Programs include school for students and preschool children on weekdays, academic tutoring on Tuesdays and Wednesdays, Bible school on Saturdays, and Bible study and discipleship for adults on Sundays. There is also a home visit program for those with special needs, and a homeless ministry in San José on Saturday mornings.
