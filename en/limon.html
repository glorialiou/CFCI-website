Coordinator: Ana Matarrita
E-mail: ana.matarrita@gmail.com

Limón's ministry supports churches hoping to transform the province with the highest rate of crime, drugs, prostitution, and occult in Costa Rica. Programs focus on evangelization, spiritual warfare, and leadership training with the hope of achieving safer communities. The Limón ministry also offers seminars directed at the current needs of churches.
