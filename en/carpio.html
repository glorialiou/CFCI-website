Renuevos in La Carpio is a program for the prevention of family violence, drugs, and gang and criminal activity. To carry out our mission, we have several classroom buildings and sports facilities for programs emphasizing education and goal-setting. Individuals are also provided with study skills, career guidance and counseling, and working techniques for high risk groups.
