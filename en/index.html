We are CFCI Costa Rica

Christ for the City International (CFCI) is an international missions organization dedicated to the service of others in the name of Christ. We have over 350 employees in 16 countries serving more than 70 ministries. We invite you to share hope with the world and to follow what God has called you to do.

Seek Opportunities

We encourage you to view our website and explore opportunities for service. CFCI provides many ways for you to use your gifts of hope to help others.

Reach Out To Us

Check out the photos on our Facebook page.
YOU could be in these photos! We invite you to learn about the experiences and stories of our volunteers and missionaries who have given themselves to bring hope to others in the name of Jesus.

Don't forget to like and share our page with your church, family, and friends!
