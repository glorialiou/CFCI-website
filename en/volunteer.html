BECOME A MISSIONARY

Our missions team receives missionaries from around the world that work shoulder to shoulder with the different ministries around Costa Rica. Missionaries offer their support and service building churches, developing programs, evangelizing, and working with children.
There are different opportunities depending on the length of your work:

  • Short-term Missionary (1 month-1 year)
  • Extended Short-term Missionary (1-2 years)
  • Intermediate-term Missionary (3-4 years)
  • Long-term Missionary (5+ years)

Contact our office and visit the CFCI website for more information.
